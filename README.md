# Realism: Resourced

(First released mod in the wild, kinda nervous tbh.)

Hi! If you're reading this, thank you for being interested in the mod!
Realism: Resourced is a gameplay mechanic mod focused on bringing immersion and realistic depth to standard Minecraft systems, completely reshaping how you progress and craft.

### 🌟 Key Features:
* **Realistic Crafting Overhaul:** Recipes have been balanced to make sense. For example, creating paper or crafting specific items now requires more realistic steps and materials, balancing the quantity of the results!
* **Unique Items:** Adds new custom items to the game, including **THE GOD APPLE**, to enhance your survival experience.

If you find any bugs, let me know in our issue tracker! This is my first time publishing a mod, so your feedback is extremely valuable to help me improve it.
